# Product Glossary

## Organization

A group that owns events.

Examples:

Church

Company

Family

School

Association

NGO

---

## Workspace

The environment where an organization manages its events.

---

## Event

A scheduled gathering with attendees, activities, and objectives.

---

## Guest

Anyone invited to an event.

---

## Attendee

A guest who registers or checks in.

---

## Organizer

A user responsible for planning or managing an event.

---

## Vendor

A business or individual providing services for an event.

---

## Check-in

The process of confirming attendance.

---

## Event Page

The public webpage for an event.

---



## AI Session

A conversation between the organizer and EventPilot AI.

---



## Organization Owner

The user with full administrative control over an organization.

---



## Team Member

A user with delegated permissions within an organization.

---



## Event Template

A reusable event configuration that can be used to create future events quickly.
