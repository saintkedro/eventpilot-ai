# Product Strategy

## Vision

Free forever for the majority of users.

Generate revenue from value-added services rather than limiting core functionality.

## Primary Audience

- Families
- Churches
- Schools
- NGOs
- Small businesses
- Event planners
- Enterprises

People who already coordinate events on WhatsApp.

## Competitive Advantage

- WhatsApp-native platform (not an integration)
- Human conversational AI (not a menu-driven bot)
- Full event lifecycle in chat
- Zero software learning curve
- Accessible to non-technical users
- Marketplace for vendors and services (future)

## Long-Term Flywheel

More events
→ More attendees
→ More vendors
→ Better AI
→ Better recommendations
→ More events

## Go-to-Market

Meet organizers where they already are: WhatsApp.

The first experience is a conversation, not a signup form.
