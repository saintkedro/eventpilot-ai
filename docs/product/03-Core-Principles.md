# Core Product Principles

## 1. WhatsApp Is the Platform

Event management happens in WhatsApp. If a workflow can be completed in chat, it should be.

---

## 2. Conversation First

Every major workflow begins with natural language. Never force users through unnecessary forms.

---

## 3. Human, Not Robotic

EventPilot must feel like a capable coordinator — warm, context-aware, and proactive. Not an IVR bot or a rigid menu tree.

---

## 4. AI First

AI is not a feature. AI powers understanding, planning, recommendations, and automation throughout the event lifecycle.

---

## 5. Mobile First

Everything must work beautifully on a phone. WhatsApp is inherently mobile — the product must match.

---

## 6. Web Is Supporting

Public event pages, payments, and guest flows exist only where WhatsApp is insufficient. The dashboard is optional.

---

## 7. Accessibility

Everyone deserves to use EventPilot.

- WCAG AA compliance
- Keyboard support
- Screen reader friendly

---

## 8. Security

Privacy is non-negotiable.

- Least privilege
- Encrypted data
- Secure by default

---

## 9. Simplicity

Remove clicks. Remove friction. Reduce cognitive load.

---

## 10. Every Guest Matters

Guests should receive a thoughtful, polished experience from invitation through post-event follow-up.
