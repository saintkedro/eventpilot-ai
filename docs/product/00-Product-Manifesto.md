# EventPilot Product Manifesto

Version: 2.0

Last Updated: July 2026

---

## Why EventPilot Exists

People already organize events on WhatsApp.

They create groups. They send flyers. They chase RSVPs. They lose track of who is coming. They stress on event day.

The problem is not that people lack tools. The problem is that event management is scattered across chats, spreadsheets, links, and phone calls.

EventPilot exists to turn WhatsApp into a complete event management platform.

---

## Our Belief

Everyone is already on WhatsApp.

Event planning should not require learning new software.

It should feel like messaging a capable, friendly event coordinator who remembers everything and keeps you on track.

Not a bot with menus.

Not a dashboard with forms.

An assistant.

---

## Our Vision

Organize any event through a simple conversation on WhatsApp.

---

## Our Mission

Build the world's most accessible event management platform — one that lives where people already are.

---

## Product Philosophy

**WhatsApp is the platform.**
Not an integration. Not a notification channel. The product.

**Conversation is the interface.**
Describe your event naturally. No forms unless absolutely necessary.

**Human, not robotic.**
EventPilot should feel like a person helping you plan — warm, clear, proactive, and context-aware.

**Every guest matters.**
Invitations, reminders, and follow-ups should feel thoughtful and personal.

**Automation without losing control.**
EventPilot handles the work. The organizer stays in charge.

---

## North Star

If someone who has never organized an event before can set up and manage one entirely through WhatsApp — without opening a dashboard — we have succeeded.

---

## Long-Term Goal

Become the default way the world organizes events — through conversation on WhatsApp.
