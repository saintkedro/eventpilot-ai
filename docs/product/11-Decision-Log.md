# Product Decision Log

Every significant product decision must be documented here.

---

## Decision 001

**Title:** AI First

**Decision:** Every major workflow should begin with AI assistance.

**Reason:** Most event software expects users to understand event management. EventPilot should teach while helping.

**Date:** July 2026

---

## Decision 002

**Title:** WhatsApp Native

**Decision:** WhatsApp is a first-class interface.

**Reason:** Millions of organizers already coordinate events through WhatsApp. Instead of asking users to leave WhatsApp, EventPilot should meet them where they already are.

**Date:** July 2026

---

## Decision 003

**Title:** Free Core Platform

**Decision:** The majority of users should never need to pay. Revenue will come from premium capabilities and marketplace services.

**Reason:** Growth is more valuable than early monetization.

**Date:** July 2026

---

## Decision 004

**Title:** Conversation Before Forms

**Decision:** Natural language is the preferred interaction model.

**Reason:** Talking about an event is easier than filling long forms.

**Date:** July 2026

---

## Decision 005

**Title:** WhatsApp Is the Platform

**Decision:** EventPilot is a WhatsApp-native event management platform. WhatsApp is not a channel or integration — it is the product. The dashboard is optional and secondary.

**Reason:** Users already coordinate events on WhatsApp. Requiring a web app for core workflows adds friction and contradicts the product thesis.

**Date:** July 2026

---

## Decision 006

**Title:** Human Conversation Over Bot Menus

**Decision:** Primary interaction is natural language conversation with context-aware AI. Numbered menus and rigid flows are fallback only.

**Reason:** The product must feel like a human coordinator, not a traditional chatbot.

**Date:** July 2026

---

## Decision 007

**Title:** Phone-First Identity

**Decision:** WhatsApp phone number is the primary organizer identity. Email/Google auth is optional for dashboard access and account recovery.

**Reason:** The first interaction is on WhatsApp. Identity should match where users already are.

**Date:** July 2026

---

## Decision 008

**Title:** Guest RSVP via WhatsApp and Web

**Decision:** Guests can RSVP through a WhatsApp message from EventPilot or via a shareable web link. Both channels are supported from MVP.

**Reason:** Not all guests will be reachable via WhatsApp. A web link ensures universal access without forcing organizers into a dashboard.

**Date:** July 2026
