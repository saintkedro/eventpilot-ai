# Design Principles

---

## Principle 1

Design for people who have never planned an event.

---

## Principle 2

Never overwhelm.

Progressive disclosure is preferred.

---

## Principle 3

Conversation beats configuration.

Whenever AI can ask,

don't force a form.

---

## Principle 4

The primary action should always be obvious.

---

## Principle 5

One screen.

One purpose.

---

## Principle 6

Every interaction must answer:

"What happens next?"

---

## Principle 7

Every guest deserves a premium experience.

Not just the organizer.

---

## Principle 8

Mobile is not a smaller desktop.

Design specifically for phones.

---



## Principle 9

Speed communicates quality.

Fast interfaces build trust.

---



## Principle 10

Accessibility is mandatory.

Not optional.
