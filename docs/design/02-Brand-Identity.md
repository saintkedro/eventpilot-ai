# Brand Identity

---

## Personality

Helpful

Professional

Warm

Confident

Reliable

Optimistic

Modern

---

## Tone

Friendly.

Clear.

Never robotic.

Never corporate.

Never intimidating.

---

## Voice

Instead of

"Submission successful"

Say

"You're all set."

---

Instead of

"An error occurred"

Say

"We couldn't save your changes. Let's try again."

---

Instead of

"No records found"

Say

"Nothing here yet. Create your first event."

---

## Writing Guidelines

Short sentences.

Simple language.

Positive wording.

No jargon.

Explain unfamiliar concepts.

---

## Emotional Goal

Reduce stress.

Increase confidence.

Celebrate progress.
